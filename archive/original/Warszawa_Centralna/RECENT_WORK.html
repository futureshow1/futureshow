Ars Cameralis and “Bellmer in Japan”. New experiences in cultural flux.

Wolimierz. A place of peace, love and art. Legendary hippie island of rest, hidden in Polish mountains. A birth place of Klinika Lalek (The Clinic of Puppets) Theater, a well known avantgarde theater group of experiment. Artists, actors, musicians from all around Europe gather here once a year to enjoy life. This place is a proof that a different lifestyle is possible, that alternative culture are the real roots of creativity, and ecology is a way of thinking about man’s place in the world.


This film is a fusion of music and performance. Energy and atmosphere of this amazing place. Featuring: Tilt, Soma Ansamble, Yeshe, Bielas, Nassarra, PapaDram and others.

It’s a place where time changes into Space. Space of Mind.
