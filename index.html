How It Works Jak pracuję
1

Your idea, my execution

You bring the subject, audience, and goals. I handle research, architecture, design and development — using Claude AI as my development partner.

Twój pomysł, moja realizacja

Dostarczasz temat, grupę docelową i cele. Ja zajmuję się badaniem, architekturą, designem i programowaniem — z Claude AI jako partnerem deweloperskim.

2

Zero dependencies

Pure HTML/CSS/JS. No frameworks, no servers, no maintenance costs. One file that runs forever on any hosting — including free GitHub Pages.

Zero zależności

Czysty HTML/CSS/JS. Bez frameworków, bez serwerów, bez kosztów utrzymania. Jeden plik, który działa wiecznie na dowolnym hostingu — w tym darmowym GitHub Pages.

3

Fast delivery

AI-augmented workflow means complex projects ship in days, not months. From brief to deployed product — radically faster than traditional development.

Szybka realizacja

Workflow z AI oznacza, że złożone projekty dostarczam w dniach, nie miesiącach. Od briefu do wdrożonego produktu — radykalnie szybciej niż tradycyjny rozwój.

Background

Visual artist, film director, and cultural anthropologist. 25+ years in experimental film, theater, and digital media — from video projections for Krystian Lupa's international productions to European Film Award nominations.

AI revolution — since day one

Participating in the generative AI revolution since 2023. Studied CS50 at Harvard for technical foundations. Continuously tracking the landscape — Matt Wolfe, Curious Refuge, Theoretically Media, and dozens of creators pushing boundaries daily.

My approach: AI curating — selecting, combining, and orchestrating the right tools for each project. Claude Code, LTX, Runway, Stable Diffusion, Flux, Suno, Udio, ElevenLabs, DaVinci Resolve, ComfyUI — and whatever appears tomorrow. The skill is knowing which combination solves each creative problem, and directing the collaboration between human vision and machine capability.
